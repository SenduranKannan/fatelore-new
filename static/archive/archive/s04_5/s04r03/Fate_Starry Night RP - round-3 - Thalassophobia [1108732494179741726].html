It was difficult to label Philoctetes a man of the sea, when compared to others who made a living off of the profession. The person himself, though, would loudly declare himself as one. A couple of fish, and then a couple of bigger fish. As far as Philoctetes was concerned, the storms and raging waves were far scarier. Things that were visible from above the surface. Even when it came to the deadly creatures that people might hypothesize to exist, the well-known ocean threats were all above the water. As long as one kept your sight peeled, and themselves well-fed, all would be good.
