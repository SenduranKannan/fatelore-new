September 18, 1999 Dear members of the Berenice Church, I hope this letter finds you well. I am Dionysius Andreas, an agent of the Assembly of the Eighth Sacrament, and it is my pleasure to make your acquaintance. I am writing to inform you of an upcoming Holy Grail War, which is estimated to take place in Berenice in approximately three months' time. While it is to my understanding that this will be the first time such an event will occur in your city, there is no need to panic. We will do everything in our power to offer assistance where necessary. As news of your nationwide power outage have reached our ears, I would like to ask whether it'd be better to keep all further correspondence between us in letter form. While email communication may be more efficient, it can also be more susceptible to disruptions should any future power outages occur. Whichever method you prefer, please let me know, and we will do our best to accommodate. With kind regards, Dionysius Andreas (edited)
